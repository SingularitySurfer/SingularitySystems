SingularitySystems is a blog about how to make information change form and how to control the various media it resides in.

While we could just call it signal processing, control theory and computer science, I think the purely technical view does not capture an important part of engineering: It is done by humans.

So while digging deep into the guts of the machines we build, we will also marvel at the beauty of the concepts, structures and patterns that emerge in the process.
